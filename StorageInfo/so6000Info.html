HPE StoreOnce 6600 Overview:

Need to take the pain out of enterprise backup and disaster recovery? You need a scalable backup solution that reduces management overheads, cuts storage costs and matches data growth. The HPE StoreOnce 6600 disk-based deduplication system is the data protection powerhouse of the enterprise data center.

It seamlessly integrates into existing SAN or Ethernet infrastructures to deliver backup, recovery and data retention through unparalleled scalability from 72 TB to 1728 TB of usable capacity, providing protection for up to 34 Petabytes of data. With speeds of up to 187 TB/hour, it offers both the capacity and performance you need to meet exponential data growth and shrinking backup windows.

Designed with no single point of failure, the StoreOnce 6600 delivers high availability backup and with HPE StoreOnce as the industry’s only federated deduplication technology, you can dedupe anywhere and control the movement of deduplicated data across the enterprise using your backup application.

Features

Industry-leading Scale-out Architecture with Unparalleled Performance for the Enterprise Data Center

The HPE StoreOnce 6600 is the pinnacle of the HPE StoreOnce Systems family, allowing you to scale seamlessly from 72 TB to 1728 TB of usable capacity (120 TB to 2240 TB raw) in a single, high-density appliance.

Pay-as-you grow by scaling up in increments of 72 TB of usable capacity and expand from 1 to 4 couplets in two standard depth 42U racks.

With the HPE StoreOnce 6600 you can back up huge amounts of data within short backup windows. High performance multi-streaming combined with HPE StoreOnce Catalyst delivers backup speeds of up to 184 TB per hour – 3x faster than the closest competitor.

Providing a Unified Solution for Seamless Movement of Deduplicated Data Across the Enterprise

HPE StoreOnce Systems are designed to meet the varying needs of the converged enterprise, from the simplicity of virtual appliances, to the price-performance efficiency of hardware appliances that span from the smallest offices to the largest data center.

Deduplicate anywhere via physical or virtual appliances - at the application source, the backup server, the target appliance or directly from 3PAR StoreServ via StoreOnce RMC.

Deduplicate where it makes sense for your business, not where technology vendors’ limitations mandate, saving time and money. Federated Deduplication with HPE StoreOnce Catalyst is available across the HPE

StoreOnce Systems portfolio – including dedicated appliances, virtual backup solutions, and on media servers and application servers. Centralize the backup of multiple remote sites, fan in up to 384 backup streams to a single fully configured StoreOnce 6600 target device.

Central monitoring of your distributed HPE StoreOnce solutions is available via HPE StoreOnce Enterprise Manager with alerting and trending to enable backup capacity management.

With HPE StoreOnce Catalyst for Simplified, Optimized Storage

HPE StoreOnce Catalyst allows a range of disaster recovery configurations, one-to-many Disaster Recovery moves data simultaneously from one site to many sites, cascaded Disaster Recovery moves data sequentially from one site to any number of other sites.

Federated Catalyst enables Catalyst stores to span nodes simplifying backup management and optimizing available storage in large environments.

HPE StoreOnce Catalyst is supported with HPE Data Protector, Symantec NetBackup and Backup Exec software through OST, Veeam, Oracle RMAN and BridgeHead Software.

Catalyst over FC provides ISV control and source side deduplication benefits of StoreOnce Catalyst via FC fabric.

HPE StoreOnce also supports critical business applications via no cost Catalyst plug-ins for Oracle RMAN, SAP HANA, MS SQL and SAP on Oracle.

For ultimately dependable data protection: Flexible, Available, Secure

HPE StoreOnce 6600 is the first highly available large scale deduplication appliance with Autonomic Restart of backup jobs. Autonomic Restart ensures that, after a failure and system failover, the backup application jobs are able to restart automatically without any personnel involvement.

Built-in redundancy at every level for no single point of failure – includes hardware RAID 6, dual fabric support for backup jobs, dual path disk arrays, a dual path internal network and dual power supplies throughout. Hot spare disk drives further minimize the impact to availability.

The HPE StoreOnce Security Pack delivers data-at-rest encryption, data-in-flight encryption and secure erase features across the HPE StoreOnce portfolio to protect data on lost, stolen or decommissioned disk drives from unauthorized access.

StoreOnce Integrity Plus checks data in the backup process, during recovery and also continually checks the data when at rest, correcting errors if necessary to avoid the risk of corrupted backups.
